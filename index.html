Here's a time line of Yasser Arafat life

  • 1929 - Born in Cairo,Egypt
  • 1944 - We are involved in his youth in the Palestinian national movement; By joining the Palestine Students Union
  • 1956 - He joined the reserve officers of the Egyptian army, and fought in its ranks since the tripartite aggression against Egypt"
  • 1965 - In the fifties he founded with his fellow Palestinian militants the Palestinian National Liberation Movement "Fatah", and declared its official spokesman.
  • 1969 - He was elected Chairman of the Executive Committee of the Palestine Liberation Organization.
  • 1973 - He was elected commander-in-chief of the Palestinian revolutionary forces.
  • 1974 - He delivered a speech on behalf of the Palestinian people before the United Nations General Assembly in New York. He received several medals and awards for peace
  • 1997 - Received the Curie Gold Medal - World Peace Council
  • 1981 - He received an honorary doctorate from the Islamic University of Hyderabad, India.
  • 1982 - He led the heroic battle against the Israeli aggression on Lebanon and the battle of steadfastness. During the siege of Beirut by the Israeli forces.
  • 1988 - Independence was declared and an independent Palestinian state was established, and he was elected as the President of the State of Palestine
  • 1993 - Chosen by the Palestinian Central Council, President of the Palestinian National Authority.
  • 1994 - Awarding the Nobel Peace Prize
  • 2004 - He died in France after being poisoned
